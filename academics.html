Bocconi Univerity

BSc in Economic and Social Sciences

Bocconi Univerity

From September 2017 until July 2020, I studied at Bocconi Univerity in Milan, completing a Bachelor's in Economic and Social Sciences. It was exciting to be in the pioneering class of this program, which, although it traces back to a long history was first taught in English and its new revised form. As such, our class benefitted from a revised curriculum including many leading edge issues in Economics, while being based on rigurous quantitative methods.

San Diego State University

San Diego State University

During the second half of 2019, I took the chance to leave Europe for the USA's sunny westcoast. Studying at SDSU was thus as much a personal as much a personal as an academic endevour.
