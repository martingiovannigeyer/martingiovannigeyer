Language and Culture

When I left my hometown for Australia in 2016 to learn English, I wouldn't have guessed that five years later, I would have worked and studied in seven countries across four continents and reach conversational fluency in four languages. In a way, one thing led to another - my experience in Australia made me confident to be able to stand on my own feet and my subsequent time in China was wonderful in introducing me to a completely different culture and mindset from what I was used. This together with my various travels, many of which included less known destinations such as Montengro or Kazakhstan led me to build a truly global mindset as well as a unique cultural competence and extraordinarily diverse group of friends.
