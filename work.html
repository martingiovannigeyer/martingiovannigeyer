Voith Industrial Services

Voith Industrial Services

Interning at Voith Industrial Services in Shanghai was an exceptional opportunity for me to both get first work experiene in a multinational enterprise, gaining an insight into various sectors such as Business Development, Operations, and HR, as well as building my cultural competence working with a diverse team of German and Chinese coworkers. Both of these competences would benefit me tremendously later on in my life.

Junge Liberale

Intern at German Liberal Party (FDP) during 2017 election campaign

Junge Liberale

I was working for the German Youth Liberals, the youth organization of the German Liberal Party during the 2017 federal elections in Germany. In a time of rising xenophobia and euro-scepticism, I found it crucial to support centrist, liberal, and pro-european ideas.

During my time in Berlin, I worked on everything from the party's social media and online presence to the organization of campaign rallies and had the great honor to meet some of the most impressive and determined people of my life, many of which now hold major political offices.

Handelsblatt

Youth correspondent for Germany's largest financial newspaper

Handelsblatt

In early 2018, I started writing for the leading German financial newspaper, Handelsblatt, reporting on the ongoing Italian election while making use of the knowledge acquired by working in politics the year prior. My articles and comments revolved around the need for political participation among members of my generation by presenting young politician from different political sides and their reasons for being active in politics.

In another article explaining the Italian election system, I highlighted the value of, as an EU citizen, being informed about politics and society in other member states as it like has an effect on ones own home country.
